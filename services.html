Digital Access

Off-Campus (Remote Access)

Our digital library breaks physical boundaries. Faculty, researchers, and students can access subscribed e-resources, journals, and databases 24x7 from anywhere globally through the Knimbus remote access portal.

Available Digital Collections

  • High-impact premium Electronic Journals from renowned publishers.
  • Comprehensive Medical and Dental Electronic Databases.
  • E-Books ranging from textbooks to deep research monographs.
  • Digital archives and back-volumes of important publications.
Registration: Initial registration for Knimbus Remote Access must be completed via this registration form. For login credentials, please contact the Central Library circulation desk.

Article Request Services

The Central Library provides dedicated full-text article request services specifically tailored to support the rigorous needs of research scholars, robust clinical studies, and our esteemed faculty members.

Turnaround Time
24 - 48 Hrs
Delivery Mode

Network Partner
DELNET

Articles that are not locally available within our subscribed databases are skillfully sourced and procured through the document delivery services of DELNET (Developing Library Network), guaranteeing you access to virtually any academic paper globally.

Submit a Request: Send your citations (including DOI or PMID) to articlerequest@yenepoya.edu.in
Circulation

Borrowing Facility

Our circulation desk is the heart of the library. Students, faculty members, and research scholars possessing electronically coded membership cards are fully entitled to borrow a vast array of library materials.

Borrowing Privileges

Member Category Book Limit Borrowing Duration
Undergraduate Students 2 Books 14 Days
Postgraduate Students / Scholars 4 Books 14 Days
Faculty Members 10 Books 30 Days
Non-Teaching Staff 2 Books 14 Days
Requirement: All students are strictly required to obtain a 'No Dues Certificate' from the library upon the successful completion of their studies.

Collections

New Arrivals

To keep pace with the rapidly evolving field of medicine and allied sciences, the Yenepoya Libraries continuously enrich their collection with the latest publications, updated textbooks, and modern reference materials.

College Wise Display

  • Yenepoya Medical College: Anatomy, General Medicine, Surgery, Pediatrics, etc.
  • Yenepoya Dental College: Periodontics, Prosthodontics, Oral Pathology, etc.
  • Nursing & Physiotherapy: Latest textbooks tailored for practical learning.
New arrivals are prominently displayed in the dedicated "New Book Display Section" at the front of the Central Library for 15 days before being moved to the general circulation stacks.

Inter-Library

Document Delivery Services

When our vast local collection doesn't hold the specific document you require, our library seamlessly steps outside its walls. We facilitate the procurement of books and specialized journal articles through robust inter-library loan programs.

DELNET Membership: We are proud members of DELNET (Developing Library Network), New Delhi. This expansive network connects us to thousands of libraries across the nation, ensuring you receive the documents you need delivered directly to our campus.
Archives

Institutional Repository

The Yenepoya Institutional Repository serves as the digital memory of the university. Developed using the robust open-source DSpace software, it meticulously collects, preserves, and disseminates the intellectual output of our institution.

Repository Contents

  • Published Faculty Research & Articles
  • Postgraduate and Doctoral Theses
  • Student Dissertations
  • Previous years' University Examination Question Papers (UG & PG)
