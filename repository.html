University Reports

Annual Reports

Annual reports detailing the Central Library's growth, activities, resource additions, and overall academic support performance over the years.

Annual Report 2023–24

The 2023–24 report covers new database subscriptions, e-resource usage statistics, user training programs conducted, and infrastructure upgrades including the digital lab expansion.

Annual Report 2022–23

Highlights include the introduction of MyLoft remote access, Grammarly Premium subscription, and significant growth in the IRINS faculty publication profiles.

Annual Report 2021–22

This report documents post-pandemic library recovery, the launch of URKUND plagiarism detection, and a record number of user education webinars conducted via virtual platforms.

Annual Report 2020–21

Covers the library's response during COVID-19, including 24×7 e-resource availability, virtual orientation programs, and remote access enablement for all users.
