DSpace Institutional Repository

The Institutional Repository has been developed using the open source digital library software DSpace. It serves as a central digital archive where members of the Yenepoya University community can access valuable academic and research outputs, including faculty publications, theses, dissertations, and previous years' question papers for both undergraduate and postgraduate courses.
