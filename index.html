About Yenepoya Libraries

A University Library That Grows With You

With a modest beginning in 1992 as a college library, Yenepoya Libraries has evolved into a full-fledged university library. Our central library — located in a standalone, air-conditioned building — serves as the information hub for students, researchers, and faculty across all faculties. All operations are computerized with an Integrated Library Management System (Koha) and biometric access control.
