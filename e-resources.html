Academic Publications

E-Journals

ProQuest Central
Health & Medical Collection E-Journals

The Health & Medical Collection is a comprehensive medical information resource for researchers, students, faculty, and healthcare professionals. Inside, users will find biomedical information from MEDLINE® alongside more than 4,200 scholarly journals, trade publications, magazines & newspapers, and over 1,000 evidence-based systematic reviews.

EBSCO
Dentistry & Oral Sciences Source E-Journals Database

Dentistry & Oral Sciences Source offers an extensive collection of important full-text journals, as well as dozens of full-text monographs. The collection includes more than 260 full-text journals, including many of the top-ranked titles in the discipline.

Digital Library

E-Books Collections

PROQUEST E-Books (Academic Complete)
ProQuest E-Books Collection

With Academic Complete, libraries can offer an affordable, critical mass of over 232,000 multi-disciplinary ebooks with unlimited, multi-user access, powerful research tools, and DRM-free chapter downloads. Academic Complete is ProQuest’s flagship subscription database that supports a wide range of students and faculty.

Clinical Key Student
Elsevier E-Books for UG Students

Clinical Key Student is an interactive educational platform designed for medical and healthcare students. It offers access to a vast collection of textbooks, multimedia resources, and clinical tools to enhance learning. Features include personalized study plans and note-taking.

Clinical Key Flex
Elsevier E-Resources for PG and Research

Clinical Key Flex is a comprehensive medical resource platform designed to provide institutions with flexible access to Elsevier’s extensive collection of medical content, including journals, books, videos, and evidence-based guidelines.

Jaypee E-Books
Jaypee Medical Content

Jaypee E-Books is a comprehensive digital platform offering access to a wide range of medical and healthcare-related textbooks covering numerous specialties, including medicine, surgery, dentistry, and nursing.

Networked Resources

Other Electronic Resources

DELNET (Developing Library Network)
Resource Sharing Network

This is a Resource sharing network between member libraries. It is a single window layer which encourages the users to explore the networked library resources. It facilitates Inter Library loan (ILL) and document delivery services among the member libraries.

National Digital Library
Virtual Repository of Learning Resources

The National Digital Library (NDL) project was initiated by the Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) to develop a framework of virtual repository of learning resources. It is an all-digital 24x7 library that integrates all existing digitized and digital contents across Institutions.

Shodhganga
Ph.D. Theses Repository

The Shodhganga@INFLIBNET Centre provides a platform for research scholars to deposit their Ph.D. theses and make it available to the entire scholarly community in open access.

E-Shodhsindhu
E-Resource Consortium

e-ShodhSindhu provides current as well as archival access to more than 15,000 core and peer-reviewed journals and a number of bibliographic, citation and factual databases in different disciplines from a large number of publishers.
